Business Profile

Our Goal is

To be an innovative company that is constantly learning, deticated to consistent development of high end software products, which are distinguished for their quality and have the best value among competitors in the same price category.

The company was founded aiming the creation of innovative, high quality industrial software, quality - price relationship. The result is a big amount of awards which the company gained abroad. Having always in mind the value, innovation, quality and style, Geneico has now been developed beyond software, covering every aspect of Product Development in the industrial support area.

To embrace new dynamically evolving technologies, it was created a system for Development of New Products, Geneico Lean-PMI. Our goal is: "Shortening the time from the Idea to Production". This is achieved by integrating the procedurs of top industrial unions and certifications, such as PDMA, AIPMM, PMI, Lean Six Sigma, TOC, AES, ALMA, CEA.

Our vision is

To achieve a worldwidely leading potision , at B2B and B2C market and at collaborations, that could lead to the future, strarting by: Geneico C.L.E.A.R

Creativity – Learning – Excellence – Advancement – Reliability
