Geneico Label Printer (GLP) - Labeling software

Suitable as a standalone printer, as a set printer or for weight and traceability-related products. Geneico Labeling software, combined with Laumas weighing technology is the ideal starting kit for manual labeling of products. GLP is suitable for label rolls up to 127mm in diameter. Consequently, the upgrade time is reduced - especially with large labels. The printers are network compatible and can also be controlled directly over the Internet. Printing unit settings and maintenance can be done without the need for tools - reducing costs and increasing print quality. New computer-based hardware provides more space and memory for faster data processing and label printing.
